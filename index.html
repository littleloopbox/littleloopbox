How It Works

  1. Order Your Box: Choose Small ($89/yr, 0-6m), Medium ($139/yr, 6-18m), or Large ($199/yr, 18m-4T). We send it prepaid.
  2. Schedule Pickup: Loop Mamas pick up from your porch on the 1st or 3rd Saturday. No shipping hassle.
  3. Store & Access: We catalog every item with photos in your app (size, gender, season). Request back anytime.
  4. Donate When Done: One-tap Blessings Box — get $30-50 credit, we give to families in need.
